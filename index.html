About Us

Immigration General Services LLC is built on the belief that immigrants deserve more than just legal paperwork—they deserve respect, understanding, and support from people who know their journey. We offer a warm, inclusive environment where our clients can receive comprehensive immigration support from start to finish.

We connect you with trusted attorneys, certified translators, trained paralegals, and public notaries. With us, you don’t have to search elsewhere. We are immigrants helping immigrants.
